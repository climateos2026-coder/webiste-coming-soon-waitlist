## Why CleanGrid Matters

A 50-household solar microgrid sounds like success — for about 18 months. Then panels underperform, batteries undercharge, and revenue models break. The hardware works. Management fails. CleanGrid fixes that.

## The Challenge

Build a dashboard for rural microgrids that tracks generation vs consumption, predicts maintenance needs, and produces financial summaries for panchayat treasurers.

## Datasets Provided

| Dataset | Source | Format | Notes |
|---------|--------|--------|-------|
| Solar irradiance | NREL NSRDB | API | Hourly, global |
| Sensor data (synthetic) | ClimateOS | JSON | Sample dataset |
