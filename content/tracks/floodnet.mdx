## Why FloodNet Matters

The world has sophisticated satellite flood-monitoring systems. And yet the people who need this data most — village leaders, district officers, families in low-lying areas — often learn that a flood is coming when they hear the water. The gap is not data. The gap is the last mile.

## The Challenge

Build a system that ingests publicly available satellite precipitation and river flow data, generates a 48-hour flood risk alert for a specific district or watershed, and delivers it via a channel that works without a smartphone — SMS, WhatsApp, or a low-bandwidth web interface.

## What Success Looks Like

- Accurate alerts that trigger real action
- Delivery to non-smartphone users
- Integration with existing community systems

## Datasets Provided
