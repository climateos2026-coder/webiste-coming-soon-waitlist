## The Open Track

You've identified a climate problem that doesn't fit neatly into our five tracks. It has real-world data behind it. It affects real communities. And you have a concrete idea for a deployable tool.

## Requirements

Your problem statement must be supported by evidence — news sources, NGO reports, peer-reviewed data, or original user interviews. Open track teams have heavier weighting on problem validation.

## What Success Looks Like

- Clear problem definition with evidence
- Deployed tool that solves the problem
- Documentation for others to use or extend
