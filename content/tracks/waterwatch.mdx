## Why WaterWatch Matters

70% of groundwater monitoring wells are still recorded manually, on paper. By the time data is digitised, the aquifer continues declining. WaterWatch builds the intelligence layer this data deserves.

## The Challenge

Build a pipeline that ingests GRACE satellite data and well records, generates monthly depletion risk scores, and presents findings for journalists, NGOs, and water board officers.

## What Success Looks Like

- Depletion scores updated monthly with uncertainty bands
- Downloadable reports for public hearings
- Alerts via webhook/email
- Works in low-bandwidth environments
