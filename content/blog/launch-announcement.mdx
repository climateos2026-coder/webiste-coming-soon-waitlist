We're excited to announce ClimateOS 2026, our global online hackathon focused on building deployable open-source climate tech solutions.

## Five Urgent Challenges

This year we're tackling five critical climate domains with measurable impact potential:

1. **HeatShield** - Urban heat island mitigation
2. **FloodNet** - Flood early warning systems  
3. **FarmFuture** - Climate-adaptive agriculture
4. **CleanGrid** - Rural microgrids
5. **WaterWatch** - Groundwater conservation

Plus one **Open Track** for participant-defined challenges.

## Dates & Registration

- **Applications open**: July 21, 2026
- **Pre-hack week**: October 1–9, 2026
- **Hackathon sprint**: October 10–12, 2026
- **Finals & awards**: October 17, 2026
